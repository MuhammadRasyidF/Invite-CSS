Our Story

Loid receives an urgent mission to recover stolen treasures. After Loid finishes his mission, he went to the party and accidentally introduces himself as Yor's husband. After a period of time, they leave the party. Loid apologizes for what he said and Yor tries to propose an idea, but she got interrupted by a car slamming into them. Loid convinces that their pursuers are patients suffering from hysteria. While running, Yor proposes that they get married as an extension of his condition. Loid agrees and tries to find the diamond ring to propose to her but discovers it to be lost. He notices a nearby grenade and grabs it, he pulls the pin and hurls the grenade at the smugglers while using the pin as a makeshift engagement ring
